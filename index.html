// Featured Project
Urban Rhythm: Predicting City Mobility with Temporal Graph Networks
Applied temporal graph neural networks to NYC taxi dataset (500M+ records) to model how human movement patterns shift across time. Achieved 18% improvement over LSTM baselines with interpretable attention weights.
